Our Mission

To preserve and promote the rich cultural heritage of South Indian performing arts while making a meaningful impact on the lives of underserved communities in Tamil Nadu.

Preserve Traditions

We train young artists in folk forms such as Parai, Karagattam, Oyilattam, and Kummi, ensuring that ancestral rhythms continue to inspire future generations.

Empower Communities

Performances and workshops raise funds that directly support folk artist communities across Tamil Nadu with educational supplies, food security, and healthcare access.

Build Connection

Through storytelling, dance, and percussion we connect the Tamil diaspora with their cultural roots, fostering pride and shared identity around the globe.

Signature Programs

Dynamic performances and outreach initiatives celebrate folk art and uplift the artists who keep these traditions alive.

Parai Percussion Ensemble

High-energy drumming showcases the heartbeat of Tamil culture with layered rhythms, traditional chants, and storytelling.

Karagattam & Folk Dance

Graceful balancing acts and folk dances honor agrarian rituals and the resilience of rural artistry.

Community Workshops

Interactive sessions introduce youth to folk instruments, costumes, and history while nurturing confidence and cultural pride.

Community Impact

Every performance supports livelihood projects for artists and their families in Tamil Nadu. Together we safeguard an artistic lineage that spans generations.

  • 150+ Artists Sponsored
  • 30 Community Performances Each Year
  • 12 Villages Supported in Tamil Nadu
